Why VC QuickSubmit?

VC QuickSubmit speeds up the workflow for submitting an assignment to Moodle.
Instead of going through Google Drive or Dropbox to turn an assignment in, you can submit it to Moodle directly through VC QuickSubmit.

EASY TO USE

Intuitive features such as drag and drop, and "Open In" from iOS make VC QuickSubmit the easiest way to submit a file to Moodle.

DRAG AND DROP

With iOS 11 you can drag and drop files from supported apps into VC QuickSubmit to speed up your workflow.

Multiple Files

VC QuickSubmit supports turning in multiple files using Moodle, just drag and drop or share some files into Moodle and click Continue once you have all your files imported!
